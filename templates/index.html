Pulse Support

Medical Tech

My product, Pulse Support, is a revolutionary device designed to provide immediate assistance in emergency situations. The concept behind Pulse Support is simple yet groundbreaking. By placing the device on a person, it utilizes advanced scanning technology to quickly and accurately assess their body for any injuries or medical conditions. The device performs a comprehensive body scan, capturing vital information about the person's health status.

Once the scan is complete, the gathered information is instantly transmitted to first responders, medical professionals, and hospitals. This real-time data transmission enables immediate awareness and informed decision-making for the individuals involved in the person's care. Whether it's a paramedic on the scene or a doctor at a hospital, they will have access to crucial details about the person's injuries or medical conditions, allowing them to provide timely and effective treatment.
